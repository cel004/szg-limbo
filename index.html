Exhibition series

Limbo.com

April 4 2026 / 12pm – 8pm

at Lychuu Baldwin (41 Baldwin St.)

Flashbacks to flash games and game lobbies, flashes of a space that seemed cluttered but wasn't really. What was your first username? What did we play? Where were we when the internet was unfamiliar and the real world full of color? I’ll see you on the playground, then on game; I’ll meet you in a place full of bliss. What will you find in limbo, in between dreams and reality?

Inspired by flash game lobbies and early memories of the internet, limbo.com is an offline exhibition exploring memory of a time when our physical worlds had only begun extending into the metaphysical space of the internet. In this limbo, we ask the audience if this past reality is only a dream now.
