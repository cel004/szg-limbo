Fish & Frog

2026

50 × 30 cm

Acrylic on canvas

Two games that defined my childhood are Feeding Frenzy and Zuma. As fun and memorable as they were, I cannot recall the last time I played them. And ever since then, these games are in limbo in my head, occasionally popping up as nostalgia.
