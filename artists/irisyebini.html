8M Deep

2026

I've been training as a competitive swimmer since 2005. Many of my dreams and memories are submerged in water—I always felt like I was becoming a sea creature. Twenty years have passed, yet the memories of water remain vivid in my mind. This year, feeling detached from reality, I returned to swimming. I spent time in a kind of limbo, moving through stressful waters to find relief. Welcome to my space. This space simulates my headspace: an aquarium where you can discover which sea mammal resonates with you, and a personal memory from the time I hit my head hard on the bottom of a swimming pool in China—waiting endlessly in that vast, echoing space. Don't forget to take a picture on the PC before you leave.
